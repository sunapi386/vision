# Chapter 6: The Marketplace

The previous two chapters described a platform and a unit of work. This chapter describes what happens when you combine them: a marketplace where domain experts build, sell, and improve AI workflows.

The concept is simple. An expert builds a workflow that automates a process they understand deeply. They publish it to the marketplace. Other organizations purchase and deploy it. The expert earns revenue on every deployment. The buyer gets a working solution for a fraction of what custom development would cost.

This is the App Store for work. Not apps that entertain -- workflows that produce. A TN visa workflow. An SR&ED filing workflow. A medical records classification workflow. A property management intake workflow. Each one built by a domain expert, verified by production use, and available to anyone who needs it.

The economics of this model are unlike anything in traditional consulting, traditional SaaS, or the current wave of AI wrappers. This chapter examines why.
